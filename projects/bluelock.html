Blue Lock Project

Blue Lock: The NEL ARC

Me and my partner made a project that based off the anime called "Blue Lock" and more specifically season 3 of "Blue Lock". We have tried to shorten as best as possible so that it could be somewhat categorized as a movie.

A task that was somewhat exhausting and hard was creating a span for each time a team had scored and I had to do this for 4 different matches, after like 2 matches it got tiring pretty quick. Another challenge was finding similar fonts as writers had used in the anime/manga, we had went through so much fonts until we found the right few that were perfect for our Blue Lock Project.
